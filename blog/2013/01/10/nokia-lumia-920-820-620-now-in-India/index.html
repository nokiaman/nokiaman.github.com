Nokia Lumia 920, 820, and 620 Now Available in India

Finally the wait is over! Nokia has been promising and teasing India with the new Lumia phones based on Windows Phone 8 for last weeks and they are finally available for sale in India.

Nokia announced the phones in a huge press conference in New Delhi. While the 920 and 820 will be available, online and at stores, soon, the 620 will release in Februrary.

The Lumia 920 will be cynosure of all eyes as it is THE flagship phone of Nokia. Nokia has upped the hardware quotient with pure view floating lens camera, highly sensitive touch screen and wireless charging.

Flipkart has listed the White, Black, Red and Yellow variants of the 920 at Rs. 36,999. It is a preorder though and they expect it to be released in 3rd week of January.

Exciting times for Nokia fans in India!
