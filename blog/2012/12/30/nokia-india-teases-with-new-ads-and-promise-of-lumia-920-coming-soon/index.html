Nokia India Teases With New Ads and Promise of Lumia 920 Coming Soon

Nokia India is running a new Lumia 920 ad on television channels in India ( and aired during the ongoing India-Pakistan cricket series). Catch the ad below:

It asks you to “Get Ready to Switch”. Yes, we are ready, but when is the device coming, already?

Nokia India also made a post on Facebook that it will keep its New Year resolution and bring the Lumia 920 soon

While it is good to see the ads, it is still disheartening that we only get to see the “soon” and no solid date on when the device will be released in India.

Go ahead Nokia, reveal the release date and then talk about keeping up the promise, please!
